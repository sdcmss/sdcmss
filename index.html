Power of Love: The Movie

SM Cinema Cebu, April 2022

Last year, my favorite k-pop group anounced that they will be showing a movie in Cinemas worldwide. Of course, as someone who is obsessed with their music, I immediately bought a ticket for myself. I went with my friends who also wanted to watch the movie. It was one of the memorable moments of my life because I was able to meet new people and I was surrounded with people who have the same interest with me.
